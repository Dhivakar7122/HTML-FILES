About KARUDA TEA

Welcome to KARUDA TEA, where passion for tea meets a commitment to quality. Our journey began with a simple love for the art of brewing and a desire to bring the finest teas to enthusiasts around the world.

Our Story

At KARUDA TEA, our story is steeped in tradition and innovation. It all started in 2016 when M.Duraipandiyan embarked on a quest to source the most exquisite tea leaves from the world's renowned tea gardens. What began as a personal pursuit soon blossomed into a mission to share the richness and diversity of teas with tea lovers everywhere.
