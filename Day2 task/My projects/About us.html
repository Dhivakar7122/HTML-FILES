Our Story

At KARUDA TEA, our story is steeped in tradition and innovation.
It all started in 2016 when M.Duraipandiyan embarked on a quest to source the most exquisite tea leaves from the world's renowned tea gardens.
What began as a personal pursuit soon blossomed into a mission to share the richness and diversity of teas with tea lovers everywhere.

Our Mission

Our mission is clear to elevate the tea experience by offering a curated selection of premium teas that delight the senses and nourish the soul. We strive to go beyond just providing tea; we aim to create moments of tranquility and connection in a fast-paced world.
